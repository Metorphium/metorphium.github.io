Modular

AUG, 2022 - present

The concept is to modify Weapons with Nodes in a NodeGrid. Said Nodes can be unlocked and acquired throughout the Game and have various effects on the projectiles beeing shot. Different Weapons have different Base Stats and different NodeGrid Layouts. Straight Big Layouts for example allow for better customization than small short Layouts.
