Toribash Photo Studio (TBPS)

MAR, 2018 - JUL, 2018

Toribash Photo Studio is a tool to create stunning screenshots of Player customized Toris in the Game Toribash, developed by Nabi Studios and released in early 2006!



Importing

Load any Tori from Toribash on your computer into TBPS! The Tools loads the textures and supported Items in.



Scenery

Customize Lighting and Backgrounds



Post Processing

Play around with fancy post-processing effects like Bloom, Dept of Field, Color Correction, Volumetric Lighting, Vignette, Grain and Chromatic Abberation



Posing

Pose each limb individually for perfect control or select one of the pre-configured Poses



Screen Capturing

Save your Creation in many Image formats, even with upscaling to 8k!
