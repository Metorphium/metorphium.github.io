Enantiosemy

Arcade, Twin Stick, Controller Support, Singleplayer, Action, Score Attack

May, 2020 - Jan, 2021

Actually a fun, small, challenging Twin Stick Arcade Game that puts your hand coordination to the test. It's simple, destroy objects with one of your cursors of the same color, the game gets progressively harder by spawning more objcets to destroy and expanding the game field. It has an integrated Backend for Account Creation, Leaderboards, Game Modes and additional unlockable Music & Color Themes that synced to the beat.
It's a shame I didn't release it, because it was ready to be...
